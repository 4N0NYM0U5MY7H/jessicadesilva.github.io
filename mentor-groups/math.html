Mentorship Programs for Students in Mathematics

Pacific Math Alliance

The PMA is a regional community of mentors who will help underserved students pursue advanced study or a career in the mathematical sciences.

National Math Alliance

The National Math Alliance is a community of math sciences faculty and students who want to support underrepresented students in earning a doctoral degree in a mathematical science.

subGROUPS

Small online peer groups of first-year math graduate students that connect students with experiences and identities in common.

AMS e-Mentoring Network

A blog supported by the American Mathematical Society to connect students and mentors in the mathematical sciences.
